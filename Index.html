Gwent

Hello, welcome to my Web Authoring class project page. This page will be about the card game Gwent, and me playing Gwent.

Gwent is an online card game created by CD Projekt Red, a Polish game development studio. Gwent was initially a small mini game in the game 'The Witcher 3', but after many people liked it so much, CD Projekt Red decided to make it a full game of its own, and here we are about a year later, with Gwent in its closed beta. I was fortunate enough to be invited to the closed Beta.

So I'm not going to try and explain the game fully here, I could try, but realistically you will easily find much better explanations or full guides elsewhere if you're interested. I'm just going to say how I play, and give some tips to anyone who wants to give it a go

When I started, I played all four factions, but since the first few days, I have played exclusively the Scoia'tael faction, so I will show some of my decks, and talk about my play style.
